Butter Chicken 

There are three kinds of people in this world. Those who love Butter Chicken, those who think Butter Chicken is overrated and those who have never tasted Butter Chicken. Of all of these, I feel deep sorrow for the third kind. It is a loss of gigantic proportions.

Naan

A leavened flat bread often paired with butter chicken originating from northern India, which is traditionally baked by slapping the bread dough onto the side of a hot dome shaped clay oven referred to as a tandoor.

Tandoori Prawns 

The Australians have have their shrimp on the barbie, but we love a prawn in a tandoor. There is nothing better than an Indian spiced prawn cooked inside the tandoor on a skewer, the intense heat from all sides cooking the prawn quickly and keeping all the juices intact.
